SKILLS

HARDWORKING

puts in consistent effort, dedication, and diligence into their tasks or responsibilities. A hardworking person is often persistent, focused, and willing to go the extra mile to achieve their goals, often working long hours or with great intensity to complete tasks effectively and efficiently.

RESPONSIBLE

who is dependable, trustworthy, and accountable for their actions or duties. A responsible person takes ownership of their obligations, makes thoughtful decisions, and ensures that tasks are completed properly. And are also aware of the consequences of their actions and strive to fulfill commitments reliably.

TIME MANAGEMENT

Time management in school and work involves organizing and planning how to divide your time effectively between your academic or work responsibilities. Good time management helps you accomplish more in less time, reduces stress, and improves productivity.

SELF MANAGEMENT

Self-management skills refer to the abilities that help individuals control their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors to achieve personal and professional goals. These skills are essential for productivity, personal growth, and overall well-being.
